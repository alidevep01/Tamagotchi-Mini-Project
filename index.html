How To Play

Take care of your pet so they do not die!
Tamagotchi dies if you ever hit 1 or 10 in any category.
  • Feed when hungry, but not too much.
  • Turn off lights to ensure they get adequate sleep.
  • Play with your pet so they do not die of boredom.
